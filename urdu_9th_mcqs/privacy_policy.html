Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2025

Thank you for choosing to use our 9th Class Urdu Book Interactive Learning App. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Our App is designed to minimize data collection. When you access our App, we may collect the following types of information:

1.1 Device Information:

  • Basic device information: device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (only as required for app functionality)
  • App performance data: crash reports and system logs (to improve app stability)

1.2 User-Generated Data:

  • Progress data: quiz scores, completed chapters, and learning progress
  • Usage data: features accessed and interaction patterns

Important: All user-generated data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store this information on external servers.

2. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

We use the minimal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our App functionality
  • To track your progress through the curriculum (locally on your device only)
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

3. GOOGLE ADMOB

Our App uses Google AdMob, a mobile advertising service provided by Google, to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to deliver personalized advertisements within our App.

3.1 Information collected by AdMob:

  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Geographic location (approximate)
  • App usage data

3.2 AdMob Privacy Practices:

AdMob's use of information collected from our App is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. For more information on how Google uses collected information, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.3 Ad Personalization:

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your Android device settings:

  • Go to Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of personalized advertising

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information:

  • All user-generated data is stored locally on your device
  • No user data is transmitted to external servers (except as noted for AdMob functionality)
  • The app uses standard Android security practices to protect local data

5. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our App is intended for educational use by students in 9th grade, which typically includes children under the age of 18. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children.

5.1 Information Collection from Children:

  • We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children
  • All learning progress data is stored locally on the device only
  • The only external service used is Google AdMob for displaying advertisements

5.2 Parental Rights:

Parents or legal guardians can review their child's locally stored data by accessing the app on their child's device. Since data is stored locally only, deleting the app will remove all user data.

6. YOUR CHOICES

You have certain rights regarding your data:

  • All user data is stored locally on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the app
  • You can opt out of AdMob personalized advertising through your device settings

7. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our GitHub repository and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
